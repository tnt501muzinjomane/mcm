Wards and Councilors The Wards demarcations are a requirements of the Urban Government Act of 1969. As stated earlier on, they are specific boundaries that separated one community from the other in an urban area. The determining criteria dependent on the progressive development elements of each area (e.g. highly industrialised areas) or each community's demographics (suburbs, semi-rural or informal etc). Ward 1 Cllr. Nganono Manyatsi Areas Mahwalala (Zone 5 & 6) Nkwalini (Zone 2, 3 & 4) Hilltop Manzana (Zone 1) Lomkiri Township Ward Profile This ward has seen a total of over 50% of the land area formalized under the Urban Development Project (UDP). Most of the formalized plots have been formally allocated to the residents and they are in the process of securing title deeds through the 99 year lease hold title. This was one of the largest informal settlements in the City. Now this area boasts of an improved basic infrastructure and formal structures which have changed the image of the ward.
