The Council employs Chief Executive Officer or City Manager to implement the policies and dictates pronounced at Council convergences. This office reports direct to Council (and in certain instances, to the Minister). The other officers that are directly employed by the Councillors are the Director of Technical Services, Director of Corporate Services Director of Finance, Director of Community Services and Office of the CEO. These officers can only be dismissed by the Council. The rest of the managers and staff are employed at the instance of the Chief Executive Officer.
