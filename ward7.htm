Ward 7 Cllr. Sthembiso Nhlandze Areas Mbangweni Makholokholo East Ward Profile Consists of only formal settlements. All of the residents in this Ward are on the Council’s Rates Payment Registry. This Ward is only made of middle class residential properties. All the necessary infrastructure is in place. The people in this Ward receive all the most needed municipal services.
